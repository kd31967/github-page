Admission

  • For the new admission an Admission Form should be fill by the candidates / parents /guardians.
  • The form duly filled in, should be returned to the school office along with two stamp size photograph,Adhar Card, Birth certificate, T.C. & others.
  • Documents within the last date decided by the management.
  • The last date for receipt of completed application form, will be as per notification given by the school .
  • Admission is granted in a large number in Nursery but in other classes, subject to Seat available in the class.
  • Applicants found suitable for admission, will be intimated for the completion of admission formalities bydepositing the required fee and other charges along with the necessary and relevant documents.
  • Preferd age for the admission:
On 1st April Minimum Age Maximum Age
Nursery 3 Yrs. + 3½ Yrs. +
K.G. I 3½ Yrs. + 4½ Yrs. +
K.G. II 4½ Yrs. + 5½ Yrs. +
Class I 5½ Yrs. + 6½ Yrs. +

Note : Admission in above classes requires Xerox Copy of original birth certificate issued by the Registrar birth of a Municipal Body .

ABC ENCLOSURES REQUIRED FOR NEW STUDENT AT PRE PRIMARY ADMISSION: - Copies of the Under Written Certificates are required for the final admission .

  1. Adhar Card
  2. Birth Certificate (Photostat)
  3. Caste Certificate ( For ST/SC/OBC)

Enclosure Required for Class I and above are as follows :-

  1. Adhar Card
  2. Birth Certificate
  3. Caste Certificate
  4. Marksheet of previous Class.
  5. Transfer Certificate of Previous School is Compulsory.

Note :-

  • No admission will be validated till the above mentioned documents are submitted.
  • Even if the admission fee is remitted, the admission will be only provisional till the above written documents are deposited and found correct by the school authorities.
  • The form issued by the school is non transferable & should be used original

SCHOOL MEAN:-
School is a PARTNERSHIP between teachers, students, parents and the community. Let us all work together to make our children successful in every walk of life.

For making good Educational Environment :-

  • Any communication made by the Parent/Guardian should be addressed to the Principal .
  • For Regularity, Punctuality and Discipline take interest in our child's work.
  • We should have to check the almanac every day and note the homework and other instructions given
  • We should have to check the bags if any circular/notice/invitation etc. has been issued from the School.
  • All students are expected to join the school on the notified date and schedule time .
  • It is better and compulsory for the student to complete 90% of the attendance in the year to make him/her eligible to appear for the final examination.
  • Parents are requested to inform the school if there is any change in their address and telephone numbers for good communications.
  • Children when sick, should inform the school.
  • Parents should ensure that their wards prepare their lessons well and do their homework regularly. They are advised to check the diary on all days for any information/message from the school.

UNITY MAKING :-
The school encourages harmony and peace by giving equal importance and showing equal respect to all religions. Discrimination on the basis of caste creed, colour, religion are discouraged creating an atmosphere of unity, integrity and brotherhood.
