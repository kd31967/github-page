Principal Message

Kid’s Paradise English School is run by SARVODAYA BAL NIKUNJ EDUCATION SOCIETY. Sarvodaya means rising of each and every child of this earth, and the movement should e enlarged in all over the universe by the every personnel whether one is a parents or a teacher. It should be our duty to make the children educated alphabetically as well as scientifically. At the same time their (Children) liberty of childhood should not be ruined by any means.

School should be the place of kindness and affection as that of a kingdom of God like a Paradise. In this regard one should try to abide by the quotation of all time great French philosopher Jean Jaques Rousseau in which he suggested that a child should not be given any kind of book till the age of twelve. But today we are imposing a big lot of litrary materials on children’s shoulders. So I believe that it is a time to save childhood by minimizing the burden of litrary materials as our educational authorities are also in the way to do this and so we are trying to do.

I think we should become a child along with the children and teach them. In our school we are trying to give healthy educational atmosphere and liberal attachment to the kids so that they can feel the school a good home.

The school is a middle school and is eight years old and enlighting basic education. Because basic education is the base of education and is very much required to meet the challenges of higher education. Therefore I would like to indicate to the parents and teachers to put an eye on the basic education of their kids specially when they are studying in the middle school level.

My school is a middle school and we are fully concentrated to improve the basic knowledge of the students.

At this stage I welcome all the old students and new students of Kids Paradise English School and wish a happy and knowledgeable life in the coming sessions.

Thank you all
